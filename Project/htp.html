How To Play

  • 1. You have to navigate through the given maze and reach the end.

    2. There will be a monster randomly moving about the maze. You have to avoid it. If you touch it the game would be over.

    3. In order to win, you will have to complete the maze in a given amount of time.

    4. There will be 4 words displayed on the top of the screen, each in a certain direction- North, South, East or West.

    5. In order to move, type in the word which is in the direction you want to move in. If you spell it out correctly you will move in that direction.

    6. Thus, if you reach the end of the maze like this, without being caught by the monster and being within the time limit, you will win.
